Delicious Katsu Curry!

Home

Description

Here we have a delicious dish for glotonous people

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Golden Curry cubes
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Chop the onions and carrots
  2. Fry them
  3. Add 1 curry cube
  4. Add water
  5. Blend everything
  6. Prepare rice
  7. Plate everything together and enjoy!
